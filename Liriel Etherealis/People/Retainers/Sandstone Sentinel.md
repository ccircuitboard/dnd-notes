**ARMOR:** Medium (15 AC)
**PRIMARY ABILITY:** Strength +3 and Constitution +3
**SAVES:** Strength and Constitution
**SKILLS:** Athletics, Survival, animal handling, perception 
**SIGNATURE ATTACK:** Melee Weapon Attack: +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1 d 8  + 3 slashing damage.

#### SPECIAL ACTIONS
**Action template=** Level (amount/ or passive) (if reaction or bonus action put here): Details
**3 rd level (3/day):** Warrior of the Sands. Bonus action, Move 30 ft then can Drive a creature within 5 ft. In addition, all your melee attacks deal an additional 1 d 4 slashing damage. 
**5 th level (1/day):** Sandstone Special. Whenever retainer hits with an attack, may choose to forgo damage to trip target.
**7 th level (passive):** Sandstorm. May attack 2 times per turn. Whenever you drive with Warrior of the Sands you may move them back 10 ft, and Warrior of the Sands additional damage increases to 2 d 4 now.  And retainer may use all Martial Maneuvers.
**9 th level (passive):** Sands Fury. May attack 3 times per turn. Whenever retainer drives, they may drive 5 creatures. And they may Activate warrior of the sands 5 times per day now. Sandstone special can be used 3 times per day.
**11 th level (1/Day) (Reaction):** Desert Slash. Whenever this retainer hits with an attack, may turn that attack into a critical hit. 
**13 th level (Passive):** Unleashed. Desert Slash can now be used 3 times per day. And  due to training you gain 2 bonus actions.
