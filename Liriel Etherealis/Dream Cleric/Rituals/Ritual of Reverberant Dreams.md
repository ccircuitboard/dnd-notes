Requires a cleric of Boria or a dream based character

- Night hag's Hair
- Griffon’s Talon
- Water Herb
- 16 Candles (lit) 
- A suitable amount of space
- Night with a crescent moon

This ritual takes 30 minutes to perform. The candles are placed equal places apart, all reagents in the center, with suitable magic and power (DC 20 spell check) a suitable character creates a Magic circle that when entered, makes all creatures (up to 4) inside fall asleep. all their dreamforms pierce the veil of dreams and enter somewhere in The Sappheron Elarion.
