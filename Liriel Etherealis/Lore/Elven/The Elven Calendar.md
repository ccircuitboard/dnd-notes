The Genesis of the current modern day calendar in many ways. It was the original and most popular widespread calendar within Selacine. It had many holidays, days of historical relevance, magical and spiritual seasons, more season than there are currently. They were based on certain spiritual beliefs. It was a very complicated calendar. What season it was determined how important a day was in the Elven Calendar. Almost based on the Winds of Magic.

Y’vras-Sunday (Aurora)- a day of beauty and beginnings

Se’rras-Monday (Moonlight)- a day of romance, drama, and arts. Flips with BloodSpout during the winter.

Te’rras-Tuesday (Earthglow)- day of plant upkeep due to being named after the ground.

Dorras-Wednesday (Spirtbreak)- a day of rest

Krraas-Thursday (Bloodspout) – a day of unrest, fear (full moon). Flips with Moonlight during the winter

Aethras-Friday (Magika) – a day to appreciate. Named after the God of Bounty and the Fey, Athenia

Mordas-Saturday (Deathdate) – Signifies Death of a great Mage , seen as a universal day of ending.
